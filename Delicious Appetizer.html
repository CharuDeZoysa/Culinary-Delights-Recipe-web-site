Caprese Skewers

A refreshing and delightful appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Balsamic Glaze
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a mozzarella ball, and a basil leaf onto each skewer.
  2. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
