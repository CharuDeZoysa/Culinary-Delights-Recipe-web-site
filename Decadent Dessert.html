Chocolate Lava Cake

An irresistibly rich and gooey dessert.

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. Melt dark chocolate and butter together.
  3. Whisk in sugar, eggs, flour, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Grease ramekins and fill each with the batter.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  6. Let them cool for a minute, then serve and enjoy the gooey goodness!

Chocolate Raspberry Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 pint fresh raspberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Mix chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter, press into a tart pan, and bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat heavy cream until it just starts to boil, then pour over chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour chocolate ganache into the tart crust and refrigerate until set.
  5. Top with fresh raspberries before serving.
